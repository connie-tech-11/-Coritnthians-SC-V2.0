Social Club House

MOY Jew , Grand Bay

¬CHEZ DOUNE – This is where AFN CORINTHIANS SC took birth and belongs!

“ ki mo preparer pou zot? Ene bon curry corn ek ene calamar? Accompagner de ene bon salade!” These are the satisfying words that Doune, one of AFN CORINTHIANS SC’s participating founding members, would utter to the boys on a Thursday evening after football training. There is an inexplicable feeling when you first arrive CHEZ DOUNE, where you leave the run-of-the-mill, real-world behind and your cares just slip away. Situated in Grand Bay, a few kilometres from Corinthians’ training ground, CHEZ DOUNE has become the AFN CORINTHIANS SC’ official clubhouse. You can't beat it. All meetings, match debriefings and any official club business are held and captured there.
It is the perfect getaway for the Corinthians to keep coming back to. Chez Doune is bustling, vibrant and releases an unprecedented energy which one will find nowhere. It is meaningful to all the boys because it symbolizes not only a meeting place for a football team but the friendship that bonds all the boys and which gave birth to AFN CORINTHIANS SC. CHEZ DOUNE is not only a club house but a second home for all the boys.
