Arassen Kallee formally is currently playing the Right back position. He started playing at young age at the National Formation Center and then spent 10 years in England studying and playing too. Upon returning to Mauritius with a few friends I played with we decide to have veteran team. His proudest moment was playing at the Inter-Collage tournament and First Division league with his dad in. Murugen Ramjug

Center Back
(6)

“I was among the members who founded AFN Corinthians.” Murugen describes a moment when he was playing a friendly game with his mates and later the question was posed. Why not make a club? In his younger days, Murugen played with 3 time champions of Grand Baie Young Devils Football Club. He was captain of the team and is the current vice-captain of AFN Corinthians. “Soccer is a collective game and mentality is key.” Murugen has worked for 28 years in the civil service and wants to play football even after retirement.
