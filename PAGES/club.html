A Message From Our Founders

We are delighted to be the founders of the AFN Corinthians SC. We wish to thank everyone for their contribution in making this happen but most particularly to Nicholas Thomas of OFIM and Joe Tshupula of AFN for their help on and off the field. Our message to our players and sailors is that we will run the Club to the best of our abilities to ensure that we do not deviate from our core values of respect, fair play and integrity. Above all, we want to run a club where members of the community can participate without any discrimination or prejudice of any kind. We want to grow a fan base by creating a safe and conducive atmosphere full of fun. We try to connect and interact with our sportsmen to encourage participation in the Clubsâ€™ affairs. To the Club sponsors and AFN, our message is that the Club will strive to be ran based on best good governance principles of Accountability and Responsibility. As a club, we believe that good governance is essential if the club is to be managed effectively and to survive in difficult economic circumstances affecting sport participation in the northern region of Mauritius. As a club, we will ensure that we are well complied with all the club licensing rules of MFA and MYA licensing system and will always act in accordance with the Sports Act 2016. On the football side, we are aiming to create a private veteran football league and we invite other partners who would like to be associated with our vision to join us. For the future trajectory of the club, we will be anchored on our strong desire to invest in AFN infrastructure in Labourdonnais that can accommodate a home ground and a clubhouse to be enjoyed by all sports participants and their families. On the sailing side, we want to state that we are always open to investing in youth sailing because we want to leave a lasting impression and experience for our youths and ourselves. The club wants to democratize sailing in Mauritius as this will create as many opportunities as possible for the youths and the club. We are AFN Corinthians SC.
